    Repurposed agricultural waste turned into a highly durable, water-resistant upper material.

    Sustainably tapped from rubber trees, providing excellent grip, flexibility, and longevity.

  • Organic Cotton Lining

    Highly breathable, incredibly soft, and grown entirely without harmful pesticides.

The Derby is our take on the classic everyday shoe. Redesigned from the ground up by podiatrists to support your natural foot shape, while utilizing innovative plant-based materials. Perfect for work, weekends, and everything in between.

  • Upper: Bio-based vegan leather (corn & apple waste)
  • Lining: Organic cotton & bamboo blend
  • Outsole: Natural rubber & recycled cork
  • Care: Wipe clean with a damp cloth. Do not machine wash.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

Return Policy

This policy applies to all online purchases via www.mk.gd.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer free returns and exchanges within Australia and New Zealand, so you can try your shoes at home with peace of mind.

You have 14 days from delivery to request a return or exchange.

To be eligible, shoes must be clean, in original condition, and tried indoors only for fit and comfort. Shoes worn outside can’t be accepted. Please return your pair in the original box.

How to Start a Return or Exchange

To begin a return or exchange, please email returns@mk.gd with your order number and a brief description of the issue.

Our team will review your request and, if approved, arrange the next steps through Shopify. Return shipping is paid by the customer, and where applicable, the cost of a prepaid return label will be charged at the time of booking.

Exchange Rules

We offer exchanges and store credit on eligible returns.

During launch and back-to-school periods, replacement sizes may be sent as soon as return postage has been booked, so purchasers aren’t left without shoes.

Returned items must be lodged with the carrier within 7 days of receiving the return label. If a return is not lodged within this timeframe, we may follow up or charge the value of the replacement pair to the original payment method.

Faulty Items

If your shoes arrive faulty or develop a fault, please email returns@mk.gd with a brief description and a photo. We’ll review it quickly and arrange a replacement or refund.

International Returns & Exchanges

We accept international returns within 14 days of delivery for shoes that are unused or lightly tried indoors and in original condition. Return shipping is at the customer’s cost, and outbound shipping, duties, and import taxes are non-refundable. Because international freight carries higher cost, exchanges are processed once the original pair has been received and return postage payment has been completed, or you may choose a fast refund and place a new order immediately.

Orders are shipped DAP (Delivered at Place), meaning local duties and taxes are the customer’s responsibility and may apply again on replacement shipments. If a delivery is refused or customs fees are not paid, any return costs will be deducted from the refund.

For faulty or incorrect items, Make Good® covers all return and replacement shipping and will assist with recovering duties where possible.
